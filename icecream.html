What is new zealand style ice cream, or real fruit ice cream?

Mar 11
Written By Guest User
The most common question we get asked at Far Out is, “So what makes New Zealand ice cream different from American ice cream?” Simply put, not much! Let us explain… 
New Zealand Style Ice Cream, or more commonly known as real fruit ice cream, is a popular concept in New Zealand with the invention of a machine that takes premium hard packed ice cream with real frozen fruit and blends it together into a delicious, creamy soft serve-like treat. It's as simple as that!
Traditionally New Zealand Style Ice Cream is made with a vanilla ice cream base. At Far Out Ice Cream, we offer FIVE delicious options to choose from: Premium Vanilla, Premium Chocolate, Vegan Non-Dairy Vanilla, Vegan Non-Dairy Chocolate, and a Low-Fat Vanilla Vanilla Frozen Yogurt. You simply choose the base of your ice cream, the fruit of your choice and let us take care of the rest! We really love it and hope you do too!
